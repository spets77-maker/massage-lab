About

Hi, I'm Yulia

I’ve always been drawn to the way touch can ease both body and mind. That curiosity led me to formal training in massage therapy and to building a practice where each session is intentional and personal.

At Yulia’s Massage Lab I combine Swedish, deep tissue, and sports-oriented work so we can address what you need—whether that’s melting away stress, working through chronic tension, or supporting your active lifestyle. I keep learning and refining so you get care that’s both gentle when you need it and effectively deep when you don’t.

My space is designed to be calm and private. You’ll be heard, and we’ll work together so you leave feeling restored and rebalanced.
